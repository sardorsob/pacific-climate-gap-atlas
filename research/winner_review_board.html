Recommended Direction

The Pacific Adaptation Gap Atlas

Build a guided map-first website that lets readers move from climate signal to exposure, monitoring, and response capacity. The winning angle is not another broad climate dashboard; it is a diagnostic tool that asks where risk is rising faster than measurement and response capacity.

  • Opening claim: Pacific islands face climate burdens they did little to create, but the gap is not uniform.
  • Main interaction: choose a geography and see signal, impact, monitoring, and response cards.
  • Competition advantage: combines the moral clarity of Carbon Debt, the country-card usefulness of Digital Access, and the polished guided story of Women in the Pacific.
